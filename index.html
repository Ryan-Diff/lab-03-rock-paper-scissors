Rock, Paper, Scissors Game

You select either Rock, Paper or Scissors and then click the Play button.

Once you click the Play button, the computer will randomly pick either Rock, Paper or Scissors and compete against your selection.

Outcomes: paper beats rock, rock beats scissors, scissors beats papers. Picking the same item will result in a draw.
